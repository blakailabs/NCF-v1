# NCF-006 Governance Standard v1.0

## Status

Approved / Ratified

## Purpose

Defines authority, approvals, risk controls, accountability, escalation, access control, and audit procedures for NCF-compliant organizations.

## Governance Principles

1. Human Sovereignty
2. Delegated Authority
3. Traceable Accountability
4. Least Privilege
5. Constitutional Compliance

## Governance Hierarchy

```text
Human Leadership
        ↓
Executive Agents
        ↓
Department Agents
        ↓
Specialist Agents
        ↓
Workflow Agents
        ↓
Utility Agents
```

## Authority Classes

- A1 Advisory
- A2 Operational
- A3 Managerial
- A4 Executive
- A5 Constitutional

## Risk Framework

- R0 Informational
- R1 Operational
- R2 Customer Facing
- R3 Business Critical
- R4 Constitutional

## Approval Framework

Draft → Review → Approved → Executed → Logged

## Escalation Triggers

- Missing authority
- Missing information
- Risk threshold exceeded
- Policy conflict
- Constitutional conflict

## Access Roles

- Owner
- Steward
- Operator
- Consumer
- Auditor

## Audit Requirements

Every significant action must answer:

- Who?
- What?
- When?
- Why?
- Under whose authority?

## Governance Decision Records

Governance decisions use the format:

```text
NCF-GDR-###
```

## Prime Directive

Every action must be authorized, traceable, auditable, and accountable.
