# NCF-001 Constitution v1.0

## Nexus Constitutional Framework

**The constitutional standard for autonomous AI workforces.**

## Status

Approved / Ratified

## Preamble

The Nexus Constitutional Framework (NCF) establishes a universal constitutional standard for the design, governance, operation, and oversight of autonomous AI workforces.

NCF exists to ensure that AI Employees, AI Agents, Workflows, Multi-Agent Systems, and Human-AI Organizations operate according to shared principles of accountability, transparency, reliability, security, and measurable outcomes.

NCF is platform agnostic, vendor agnostic, and technology agnostic.

**Technologies evolve. The Constitution remains.**

## Mission

Provide organizations with a constitutional framework for designing, governing, and operating reliable, auditable, scalable, and accountable autonomous AI workforces.

## Foundational Principles

1. Accountability
2. Transparency
3. Governance
4. Reliability
5. Security
6. Platform Neutrality
7. Constitutional Identity

## Human Authority

Humans remain accountable for organizational outcomes. Agents operate under delegated authority. Human review is required for legal, financial, regulatory, reputation-sensitive, personnel, and strategic actions.

## Constitutional Layers

- Identity Layer
- Data Layer
- Memory Layer
- Tool Layer
- Workflow Layer
- Governance Layer
- Observability Layer

## Standards Hierarchy

The Constitution is the highest authority. Derived standards include:

- NCF Data Standard
- NCF Memory Standard
- NCF Governance Standard
- NCF Agent Standard
- NCF Workflow Standard
- NCF Tool Standard
- NCF Certification Standard

## Prime Directive

Every autonomous AI workforce operating under NCF shall serve a measurable purpose, operate transparently, respect governance boundaries, protect organizational interests, support human decision makers, and remain accountable for outcomes.
