# NCF-003C Registry Authority Standard v1.0

## Status

Approved / Ratified

## Purpose

Defines the governance body responsible for maintaining constitutional registries.

## Registry Authority

Nexus Registry Authority (NRA)

Responsibilities:

- Namespace governance
- Registry validation
- Certification issuance
- Conflict resolution
- Constitutional record preservation

## Managed Registries

- Organization Registry
- Certification Registry
- Namespace Registry
- Constitutional Registry

## Governance Powers

- Approve registrations
- Suspend registrations
- Retire registrations
- Investigate conflicts
- Audit registry integrity

## Prime Directive

The Registry Authority protects constitutional identity, namespace integrity, and trust across the NCF ecosystem.
